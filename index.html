About

We wear the "Made in the Triangle" badge because we are proud to conceive, to grow and to flourish our companies in the Triangle of North Carolina. We eat, sleep, work, bleed and cry with each other. We understand that success is not a zero sum game. We pledge to journey together with others like us. We are entrepreneurs.

And we love our Triangle, not just for its great universities, its cutting edge innovation, its unparalleled creativity or its spirit of collaboration. We love our Triangle because we call Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill, and everything in between, home.

What do these companies have in commmon?

  • They were built in the Triangle - Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill, or somewhere in between.
  • They prominently display "Made in the Triangle" with or without the logo in their footer that links back to this page - http://mintt.us
  • They are active with the local startup community.

Who's behind all this?

Made in the Triangle is a project by Michael. He started MINTT while working on his own company and wanted to share with the world that he was part of a rich community of entreprenuers in the Triangle.

Join us on Twitter, @wearemintt as we foster recognition of the entrepreneurial community in the Triangle.

Made in the Triangle is inspired by Made in New York City and Made in Ottawa.
